About Sanrio

Sanrio is a Japanese company known for creating some of the world’s most beloved characters. Founded in 1960, Sanrio has been spreading happiness and friendship through its charming designs and heartwarming stories. From Hello Kitty to My Melody, Keroppi, and Cinnamoroll, Sanrio characters have captured the hearts of fans of all ages worldwide. Each character has its own personality and story, inspiring creativity, joy, and imagination.

History

Sanrio was founded in Tokyo, Japan, in 1960 as a small company selling gift items. Its first big hit was Hello Kitty, introduced in 1974, which quickly became a global icon of cuteness and positivity. Over the years, Sanrio has created hundreds of characters, each with unique traits and stories. The company has expanded into toys, stationery, clothing, animated shows, and even theme parks.
